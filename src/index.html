Archipel

What is Archipel? Archipel is a team of two foreigners based in Tokyo making documentaries about Japanese creators. From gaming, manga to illustrators, their documentaries cover a wide range of fields and are all available ad-free on YouTube.

Their goal is to be able to capture as many profiles as possible in order to build an archive of Japan's creativity.
